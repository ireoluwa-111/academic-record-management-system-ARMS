Capabilities

Built for the full academic record lifecycle

From matriculation to graduation, every module is designed around how universities actually operate.

Role-based access control

JWT-secured authentication with granular permissions for students, lecturers, registrars and administrators.

Student record management

A single source of truth for biodata, enrollment history, academic standing and programme progression.

Course management

Maintain course catalogues, units, prerequisites and lecturer assignments across departments and sessions.

Course registration

Guided, validated registration that enforces unit-load limits and prerequisite rules every semester.

Automatic GPA / CGPA

Results compute grade points, GPA and CGPA instantly, with full grading-scheme transparency.

Transcript generation

Generate registrar-signed, watermarked PDF transcripts on demand — ready for download or dispatch.

Audit trail & activity logging

Every change to a record is timestamped and attributed, giving full accountability and traceability.

Analytics dashboard

Enrollment trends, performance distribution and departmental insights surfaced in real time.

Record verification

Third parties verify transcripts and certificates instantly using a secure reference code.

Process

How a record moves through ARMS

A record's journey is a real, ordered sequence — from admission to a verifiable transcript.

01

Admit & enroll

The registrar creates a verified student record tied to a programme and matriculation number.

02

Register courses

Students register approved courses each semester, validated against unit-load and prerequisite rules.

03

Record results

Lecturers submit scores; the system computes grades, GPA and CGPA automatically.

04

Verify & share

A signed PDF transcript is generated, and anyone can verify it with a reference code.
